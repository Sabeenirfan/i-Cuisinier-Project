Steak

Masterfully grilled to a flawless medium-rare, complemented by a whisper of garlic and shallots, delivering an indulgent and refined gastronomic experience.
Watch Video

Ingredients

  • 1. Salt
  • 2. Prime Steak
  • 3. Black Pepper
  • 4. Olive Oil

Directions

  1. 1. On a clean, flat surface, season the steak generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  2. 2. Drizzle olive oil over the steak, ensuring it's evenly coated.
  3. 3. Preheat a skillet over high heat until it's smoking hot.
  4. 4. Carefully place the steak in the skillet, searing each side for 3-4 minutes.
  5. 5. Reduce heat to medium and continue cooking to your desired doneness.
