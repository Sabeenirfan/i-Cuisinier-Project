Chicken Burger

Exquisite linguine, expertly al dente, enveloped in a velvety tomato reduction infused with aromatic herbs, delivering a refined Italian culinary experience
Watch Video

Ingredients

  • 1. Ground chicken
  • 2. Eggs
  • 3. Olive Oil
  • 4. Black pepper

Directions

  1. 1. On a clean, flat surface, place the ground chicken in a mound. Create a well in the center of the chicken.
  2. 2. Crack the eggs into the well, then add olive oil and salt. Using a fork, gently beat the eggs, oil, and salt together, incorporating the chicken .
  3. 3. Once the mixture becomes too thick to mix with the fork, use your hands to knead the chicken mixture until well combined and cohesive.
